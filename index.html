Dragons

The most iconic creatures of every culture in the world, scales, claws, fangs, wings, horns, tails. Dangers of nature incarnate.

The term dragon does not have a strict definition. In some cultures dragons are more snake and reptilian like. While in others it is more birdlike.

Dragons are the THE fantasy creature. Dragons are the most imaginative fantasy creature, its narrative potential and flexability allows for almost any kind of story to take place.
